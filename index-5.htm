What's happening

Recent and proposed changes to electoral laws

Changes to the Commonwealth Electoral Act 1918 have been passed by Parliament, including amendments relating to party registration, the scrutiny of Senate elections and annual disclosure requirements. Further information regarding these and proposed further changes to electoral laws will be made available progressively.
